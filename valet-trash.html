How We Do It

At Junk Raider, we've streamlined our junk removal process to ensure a seamless experience for Bank & Credit Union clients.

1

Fire-Resistant Trash Cans

Residents dispose of their household trash in their Junk Raider provided, fire-resistant trash cans.

2

Effortless Collection

These receptacles are then placed outside their residences within a two-hour timeframe prior to the appointed collection commencement. 

3

Waste Removal Experts

A Junk Raider waste management specialist is then responsible for removing the waste from the receptacles and transporting it to the on-site waste compaction facility or dumpster.
